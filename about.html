ABOUT ME

I am...

a Dublin-based artist. Seven years of studying in a School of Fine arts in my hometown immersed me in diverse impasto techniques, but discovering the magic fluidity of watercolor in 2020 was pivotal for my artistic expression. My passion for Italian style encourages me to permanently improve my skills. I invite you to experience the world through the lens of soft washes and intricate details of my paintings. <3
